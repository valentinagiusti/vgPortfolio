My story

My name is Valentina Giusti and I’m from Montevideo, Uruguay. I just finished a coding bootcamp which is a short-term, full-time intensive training program that teaches practical and job-ready tech skills with around 600 hours of theorical-practical lessons.

I discovered a new passion for programming last year during the covid lockdown, specially for front-end design, and I’m now looking for my first web-developer job experience.

I love travelling and meeting people from around the world! I’ve been lucky to travel and lived abroad one year in Italy. I’m fluent in english, italian and spanish, and know the basics of portuguese.
